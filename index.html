About Us

Children with Potential Foundation (CPF) is dedicated to empowering disadvantaged students in Malawi. We believe every child has the potential to succeed if given access to education and the necessary learning resources.

Our mission is to reduce school dropout rates by providing school supplies, academic support, and working closely with communities and teachers.

What We Do

Supporting Needy Learners

Providing uniforms, learning materials, and basic needs so students can focus on their education.

Reducing School Dropouts

Helping remove financial and social barriers that prevent students from staying in school.

Community Engagement

Working with teachers, parents, and communities to support inclusive education.
